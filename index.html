About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research (BICMR), Peking University. My work centers on Liouville conformal field theory (CFT), Liouville quantum gravity (LQG), and their interfaces with probability, hyperbolic geometry, Schramm–Loewner evolution (SLE) and conformal loop ensembles (CLE).

Research Directions

  1. Construction of Liouville Field Theory (especially boundary Liouville): Segal axioms, boundary bootstrap, representation-theoretic aspects.
  2. Liouville Quantum Gravity & Random Geometry: SLE loop measure, CLE integrability, imaginary DOZZ formula.
  3. Liouville CFT and 3D Virasoro TQFT: semiclassical limits and connections to hyperbolic geometry.
