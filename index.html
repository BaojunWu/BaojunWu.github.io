About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research (BICMR), Peking University. My research lies at the interface of probability and mathematical physics, with a focus on Liouville conformal field theory (LCFT), Liouville quantum gravity (LQG), conformal loop ensembles (CLE/SLE), and three-dimensional quantum topology.
